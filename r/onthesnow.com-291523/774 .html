Overview

Ski Sawmill has a beginner's area, tubing area, increased snowmaking capabilities, and terrain park.
A lodge with 32 rooms at base with 400 additional rooms in 14 hotels located nearby.

Inside Scoop

Cafeteria and restaurant at base, ski shop with rentals and lessons, first aid, specialty shops, and liquor all at the area. 100 percent snowmaking.
